Course Details

What You Will Learn

Web Design

  • Design Principles: User-centered, consistent, accessible, and mobile-friendly
  • UI/UX: Focus on usability, navigation, and user satisfaction.
  • Responsive Design: Ensures compatibility across all screen sizes.
  • Technologies: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like Bootstrap, Tailwind, and React.
  • Backend & CMS: WordPress, Webflow, Shopify for easy content management.
  • Performance & SEO: Speed optimization, SEO best practices, and Core Web Vitals.
  • Tools: Figma, Adobe XD, Canva for design; VS Code for development

Graphic Design

  • Typography: The style and arrangement of text
  • Color Theory: Using colors to evoke emotions and create harmony
  • Layout & Composition: Arranging elements for balance and readability
  • Imagery: Photos, illustrations, and icons to enhance communication
  • Branding: Creating a unique identity through logos, colors, and typography

2D/3D Animation

  • 2D Animation Flat, hand-drawn or digital (e.g., cartoons).
  • 3D Animation Realistic, three-dimensional models (e.g., CGI movies)
  • Stop Motion Moving physical objects frame by frame (e.g., clay animation).
  • Motion Graphics Animated text, shapes, and graphics (e.g., ads, intros).
  • Traditional Animation Hand-drawn frames (e.g., classic Disney films).
  • 2D: Adobe Animate, Toon Boom, Krita
  • 3D: Blender, Maya, Cinema 4D.

Digital Marketing

  • SEO – Optimize websites for search engines
  • Social Media Marketing – Promote on platforms like Facebook & Instagram.
  • Content Marketing – Blogs, videos, and infographics.
  • Email Marketing – Send targeted promotional emails.
  • PPC Advertising – Paid ads on Google & social media
  • Affiliate Marketing – Earn commissions through referrals
  • Influencer Marketing – Collaborate with influencers for brand promotion.
